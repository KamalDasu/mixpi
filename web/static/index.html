Recommended XR18 setup — all 18 USB channels:

To record 16 channels plus a stereo mix on the X-Air (XR18) USB, set Routing > USB Send to "Analog In 1–16" for individual tracks. Then set USB Sends 17/18 to "Main L" and "Main R" to capture the stereo mix — ensuring all 18 USB channels are used for the full session.

  • In X Air Edit → Routing > USB Send
  • USB 1–16 → Analog In 1–16  (individual mono tracks)
  • USB 17 → Main L  ·  USB 18 → Main R  (stereo mix reference)
This captures all 16 mono inputs + the full stereo mix on a single USB connection.
🎚️
Pre-fader vs Post-fader for USB 17–18 (Main L/R):
  • Pre-fader — stereo mix before the master fader; level stays constant even if master is pulled down
  • Post-fader — stereo mix after the master fader; reflects exactly what the audience hears
USB 1–16 (Analog In) are always pre-fader — fader moves never affect individual track recordings.
🔇
Muted channels are automatically skipped:
  • Channels muted on the XR18 show an M badge and are not armed for recording
  • Un-mute in X Air Edit or on the mixer surface to include them
  • Re-select your channel preset or press Apply after un-muting
